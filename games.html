SPADE

A 3D platform game following the idea of hard games that punish you for any and every mistake. You have to help Foggy reach the top but, jumping over the platforms will be a challenge because, if you fall, you have to start it all over again.

This game is still in progress and my job is to create Foggy, an engaging main character. You can see more of his development in the 3D projects section.

Try the demo here: SPADE

Mr Erazer's Revenge

A 2D platform game created for our first ever Game Jam at Virtual Soul GameGen GameJam 2021 with only 3 days to develop it.

Here, you have to help Mr Erazer to get to the final boss to have his revenge against Monsieur Lapiz and his wife. And my job was to create all the environment assets and the backgrounds.

Try the game here: Mr Erazer's Revenge

Space Combat Rush

A 2D arcade game in pixel art to reminiscence the old times. It was created as our first multiplayer game. Here, the objective is to kill your adversary and survive in a space war.

And, as for my job here, I was in charge of designing the background and all the interfaces (plus some programming).

Try the game here: SCR

The Inner Doors

A 3D mystery first-person game where you have to discover what is happening and where your decisions matter. You can get 3 different endings.

In this game, my job was to create all the visual aspects of the game (from the interface, to all the 3D assets).

Try the game here: The Inner Doors
